Samskruthi Parva

Samskruthi Parva is a festival organised by Abhyudaya Samskruthika Vihara® in collaboration with Kala Sandesha Prathishtana®, to integrate Intangible Cultural Heritage of India and to up hold the Indian traditions, mainly focusing towards both rural and urban population, especially the youth. Samskruthi Parva’s aim is to create awareness and appreciation of Intangible Cultural Heritage, Traditional, Spiritual and Religious practices.
