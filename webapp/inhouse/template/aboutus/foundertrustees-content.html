Sri K Sandesh Bhargav

Vidwan Sri K Sandesh Bhargav is a renowned terpsichoreanand an outstanding choreographer, acclaimed internationally for his own attitudes and principles towards Bharathanatyam. He was initiated into Bharathanatyam by NatyacharyaVidwan R Kannanfrom Mysore. Under his able guidance, Sandesh became one of the youngest recipients of Vidwath grade. His proliferating urge to discover the extensive areas of Bharathanatyam escalated under the guidance of internationally renowned danseuse, Karnataka Kala Tilaka,Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy. Sandesh’s inundated thoughts on Bharathanatyam spread its wings of dance towards enriching Music and meticulous Tala-Laya. His in depth studies in Tala, Laya and Avadhaana were initiated and nurtured by Guru Dr. R S Nandakumar.

As a Performer

Sandesh has performed in various prestigious and coveted festivals such as Ujjayini- Poorna Kumbha Mela, Tirupati- Brahmotsava, Kerala- Kalamandalamand also represented India in the ‘International Dance Alliance’ at Narada Gana Sabha Bhavana, Chennai. Adding to it, his stage performances include Hampi festival, Khajuraho festival, Inaugural performance in Mysore Dasara festival, Natarajothsava, Pallavothsava, Vasundharothsava, etc. Sandesh is also learning Karnataka Classical Music from Guru Dr. R S Nandakumar and has put forth this knowledge in enhancing the delicacies of dance. In his journey of Bharathanatyam, Sandesh has also stepped into another area of dance that is, Natuvangam.

As a Guru

He not only intends to put forth able artists but also responsible beings with great human aspects. He started exploring the eternal magnitude of the Indian Classical dance by performing and tutoring to a number of Classical dance aspirants through his foundation Kala Sandesha Prathishtana. Kala Sandesha Prathishtana is a firmly established Public Charitable Trust and Art foundation in Mysuru, and through his creativity, innovation and new experiments within the classical bounds, he grabbed the attention and credits of the people who have great affinity towards Indian classical dance. The inception of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana with 10 pupils was in 2004. Now, it possesses more than 300 students under 18 of its branches across Karnataka (T Narasipura, Mysuru, Bengaluru, Talakad, Chamarajanagara, Kollegala) preaching dance, nattuvangam, tala and laya. His Academy’s objective is to provide an alternate platform for the rural youth to earn their livelihood, in turn keeping Indian classical forms alive. It is a cultural academy dedicated for the preservation of traditional values of Indian art, especially in the field of Bharatanatyam and Karnataka Classical Music.

Cultural Festivals

Sandesh, organizes more than one National level festivals every passing year such asNrityopasana, Kishora Bharatha, Naadayaana and Kalabhivardhana. Nrithyopasana is exclusively meant for providing propitious stage for the artists who take dancing tutelage in his organization, in order to create for them an opportunity to get exposure and recognition

As a Choreographer & Composer

Sandesh along with his learning pursuit, has made enormous contributions by staging myriad of performances and choreographies. His venture has always distinguished itself by holding onto the strong roots of Hindu mythology. Karna, from the ancient manuscript of Pampa Bharatha, Sambhavami Yuge Yuge, from the ancient manuscript of Kumaravyasa Bharatha, Navaavaranavasini (based on Lalithsahasranama- formation of the Srichakra),Dashakanaka(manuscript in the words of Kanakadasa),Shakti Peeta Darshana(acknowledged the origin of the Shakthipeetas) and Vishnupraseeda Mahishapuravasi(Stories of Vishnu temples in and around Mysuru) were effectively put across by Sandesh in his spectacular choreography.
He holds ICWA (Institute of Cost Works Account) & CFA (Charted Financial Analyst), Professional course degrees and is the Associate Member of ‘Institute of Cost Accountants of India’. Being an Assistant Vice President in the much reputed Multi National Company Genpact India Private Limited, uncompromising Sandesh has learnt to balance both his professional and the art front by still pursuing his dance career as a Performer, Nattuvangist, Guru, Management Technician, Organizer and Art Promoter.

Dr. D. Umapathy

Founder Trustee Professor Umapathy, born on 20th June 1958, in Rayadurga(AP). He successfully completed his B.B.M and M.Com degrees from University of Mysore. He began his teaching career as a lecturer in the year 1983 at Vani Sugar Arts and Commerce First Grade College, Hiriyur. Later, he was appointed as lecturer in 1984 and currently working as associate professor in Commerce at Vidyodaya Arts and Commerce First Grade College, T.Narasipura, Mysore Dist. He obtained his Ph.D in management from Karnataka State Open University, Mysore in 2011. His area of specialisation is Cost Accounting and HRM in which he has presented papers on various topics in State, National and International level seminars and conferences. He has a vast teaching experience.

During his career, he worked as a member of curriculum development and examination at University level and Autonomous college level and also actively served as Adult Education officer, NSS officer, Placement officer. He helped students to secure employment. He is the member of Governing Council of Ekalavya Institute of Technology, Ummathur, Chamarajanagar. He has great passion towards watching classical dance and listening classical music.

Apart from teaching Commerce, was much more passionate towards Public service. His passion made him to join Sandesh’s hand to establish the Public Charitable Trust Kala Sandesha Prathishtana in 2004. He is a man with plenty of experience is being proper directions to the Trust members. And Dr. Umapathy is presently working as a Professor at Vidhyodhaya Govt. College T.Narasipura Mysore.
