Nruthyopasana

A dream of uniqueness, subject oriented performances, exploration of ancient, classics, a fine blend of dance, music, literature and other art forms conceptualized as three days festival, Nrutopasana. Since 2012, Nrutyopasana is broadcasting a universal message visualizing bharathanatyam as an element encompassing the caveat-free art forms.Nrutyopasana is a formulation of classical fine art forms imbibing the most contemporary theories such as technology, applied science, management, engineering, leadership and imparting these to budding artists. The rhythmic expression of a thought is dance. And, the worship of this celestial form of dance is our Nruthyopasana. The sole motive behind this very cogitation was to render a platform braced to exhibit the calibre of every individual who gets trained in dancing. Nruthyopasana is one of the prestigious annual festivals of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana which extends up to three days.
