Kalabhivardhana

Felicitating art virtuasity

The curtains for Kalabhivardhana were rolled apart for the very first time as a part of Decennial Celebrations of KSP, 2014. Propagation of Art embedding its beauty and value by amplifying it to a wide frequency is the motto of this festival. Accomplished artists across India who have made noteworthy contribution to the field of art are honoured with the Award of title ‘Kalotthunga’ during this fete.People who have made significant contribution in the field of Dance (any Indian Classical form) and Music (Indian Classical music inclusive of instruments) are given with the titles Natya Kalotthunga and Sangeetha Kalotthuga respectively, and a concert will be arranged for them.
