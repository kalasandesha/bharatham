Day 3, 16th October 2016:

On the third day of Kalabhivardhana, a theme based Solo Dance feature is performed by Guru Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav.

The dance feature ‘Namaami Bhagavathpaadam’ is a attempt to represent the principles of Sri Adi Shakara and his life experiences is beautifully expressed through Bharathantyam.

Considering the life of Sri Adi Shankara as an invaluable message and inspired by his principles, Dr. Jyothi Shankar has penned the lyrics for the Nrithyaroopaka and renowned vocalist Vid. Sri ShreeHarsha has composed the Music for the same. Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy, who introduced ‘Vasundhara Style’ of Bharathanatyam has finely choreographed this Solo Dance feature. Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav, the founder of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana ® has presented this Dance feature on the event of Kalabhivardhana-2016.
