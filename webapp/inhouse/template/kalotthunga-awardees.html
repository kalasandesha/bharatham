Kalotthunga award

Kalabhivardhana, a 3 days’ festival is the event of conferring ‘Kalotthunga’ award for Dance and Music streams. As the name self-explicates ‘Kala & Utthunga’, this is an award which is embellished on those artists who have served the field of Indian art and have been doing this great work, who have contributed and added value to the promotion of Indian art. Thus, two awards, ‘Natya Kalotthunga’ for the connoisseurs in the field of Indian Classical Dance and ‘Sangeetha Kalotthunga’ for the connoisseurs in the field of Indian Classical Music is conferred.

The Prathishtana, with the assistance of the Award Committee chooses Kalotthunga awardee for the year and devotes a stage performance by the awardee for the respective Kalotthunga awards. The award consists of 25,000/- honorarium, a shawl, a sandal garland, and a citation. After the recipient is conferred with the ‘Kalotthunga’ title, either the recipient or the recipient’s student must give a performance on the Kalabhivardhana stage as a symbol of manifesting their lifetime service related to the field of Art they are associated to.
