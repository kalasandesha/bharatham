Manthana

Vikasana charitable foundation® in collaboration with Kala Sandesha Prathishtana® will conduct 'Manthana' to promote rural talents. Bhava Geetha, Bhakthi Geetha, Folk dance, Folk song, Debate, Painting, group singing, Desha bhakthi geethe and other culture based competitions will be organised for school children at rural schools. Winners will showcase their talents on two-day festival 'Manthana'.
