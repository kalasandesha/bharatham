Adhvaya bharatha samprathi

#500, Opposite Ayiji Bhandar, Ganapathi temple road, Giridarshini Layout, Alanahalli, Mysore, Karnataka-570028

Adhvaya bharatha samprathi is a non-profit organization works to integrate India based on its culture, heritage and traditions, mainly focusing towards both rural and urban population especially the youth because their opportunities are rare and exposure to the traditions and culture of India is limited. Organization’s aim is to create awareness and appreciation of Indian Culture, Heritage, Traditional, Spiritual and Religious practices.

Objectives
  1. To introduce, undertake and manage any welfare, religious, educational, social and cultural activities as may be decided by the organization for the benefit of the public at large.
  2. To inculcate the spirit of national integration in the minds of the youth and rural India and initiate training programs for ensuring it.
  3. To foster and facilitate education and research and publications in various disciplines especially in areas of Indian diversified culture, traditions and heritage.
  4. To train and equip social workers and thereby moralize the importance of national integrity in their minds.
  5. To offer relief to the distressed and disabled persons of the society by sponsoring social service schemes, giving donations, scholarships, grants etc to individuals or institutions.
  6. To help in starting healing and health care units for the ailing and aged and educating the ignorant masses about common ailments and diseases.
  7. To empower women and thereby involve them in the national integration programmes.
Activities
  • Hosting national integration programs in which eminent persons motivate people towards the integration of varied culture of India through their activities.
  • Arranging competitions and other activities to the school going children in order to drive the essence of nationalism in their minds and acts.
  • Celebrating all national festivals and there by preach the society the importance of Indian history and heritage.
  • Visiting elders and schools and to create interest among the people to take active part in encouraging children to take interest in Indian culture and traditions.
  • Arranging regular visits by instructors to the centers for coaching interested persons and tapping the local talents.
  • Arranging regular camps and workshops for uplifting younger generation for national cause.
  • Providing institute scholarships and sponsorships for social activities.
  • Arranging visits and tours to the places of historical interest and national heritage.
Trustees

Objectives
  1. To create and promote art form Drama in rural areas by organizing events.
  2. To nurture and spread the message of Indian culture and values.
  3. To provide an opportunity for artists to work on contemporary and modern plays.
  4. To create a facility for artists to get train in as Playwrights and Directors.
  5. To create social awareness in people through Drama and Street plays.
  6. To conduct Workshops, Guest lectures, Seminars and Small performances provide the information about the concepts that might help people to execute Theatre accordingly.
Activities
  • Conducting sessions for the rural people to educate and to promote them in the Theatre.
  • Facilitating creative interaction between traditional and contemporary Theatre artists.
  • Arranging institutes scholarships and sponsorships for talented persons to improve their specific activity.
  • Building a library for the artists related to scripts writers, literature and language to enhance their knowledge.

#42/A, “Srinilaya”, G.B.Palya,Siddharthanagar, Mysuru-570011

To study these art forms, enjoy them and take the required step in bringing these endangered art and artists to light is one of the main reasons why this organization is brought to life. Rural developments, women empowerment, betterment of youth are chief aims of this organization.

Objectives
  1. To create awareness and appreciation about the coastal arts, heritage, traditional, spiritual and religious practices.
  2. To create interest in and promote Semi-classical dance, drama, folk- lore etc native to coastal arts of India.
  3. Organize festivals every year for the propagation of coastal arts.
  4. To introduce, undertake and manage any welfare, religious, educational, social and cultural activities as may be decided by the trustees for the benefit of the public at large.
  5. To offer relief to the distressed and disabled persons of the society by way of donations, scholarships etc. to the individuals or institutions.
Activities
  • Establish an academy of arts, and organize festivals that provide platform for performance of coastal arts.
  • Conduct detailed study of diminishing coastal arts and work for their progress.
  • To encourage startups that promise to engage in recreational activities related to coastal arts.
  • To undertake projects for intellectual development, development of leadership skills in orphanages for mutual benefits and bringing out the best in them.
