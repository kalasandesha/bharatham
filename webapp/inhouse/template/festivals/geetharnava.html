Geetharnava

Swarashraya Gana Sabha® in collaboration with Kala Sandesha Prathishtana® presents 'Geetharnava', an annual music festival. This festival has been crafted solely to promote Indian Classical Music- Vocal as one of the objectives of the organisation. It aims at providing an unbiased platform for
both Karnataka and Hindustani classical music. It is an attempt to portray and thus drive the mass towards class through Indian music. Eminent virtuosi are offered a stage through Geetharnava for their music concerts every year.
