Karavali Koushalam

Karavali Koushalam is a platform created to nurture the coastal arts of India. Its main objective is to bring out the coastal talents which is losing its importance in today's era of modernization!. This cultural festival is organised by Karavali Kalanikethan® in collaboration with Kala Sandesha Prathishtana®
