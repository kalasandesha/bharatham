Bharathanatyam Marga – by Vid Sri K Sandesh Bhargav
The commencement of Kalabhivardhana was accomplished by the performance of the Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav, senior disciple of Karnataka Kala Tilaka Dr.VasundharaDoraswamy. The long recital holdsthe traditional base of Solo Dance Feature “Sambavami Yuge Yuge”. The highlight of this portrayal clings to the character ‘Krishna’ from the very famous Kumaravyaasa Bharatha, beautifully designed into a Solo Dance Drama. The process of bringing about Krishna’s ‘simple approach with strong message’ lucidly makes Kumaravyasa stand out.Sandesh has wonderfully presented the Solo Bharathanatyam Recital “Sambhavami Yuge Yuge”. His soothing performance with extraordinary grace on face, energetic Nrutta and charming expressions stolen the heart of audience. This was the highlight of the performance by Sandesh Bhargav.
