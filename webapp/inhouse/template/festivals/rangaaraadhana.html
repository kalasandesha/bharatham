Rangaaraadhana

Chiranthana Ranga Peeta® with the co-sponsership of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana® conducts the annual festival Rangaaraadhana. This festival is organised to platform for artists to build interest in and to promote Indian Theatre forms, to create a wider perspective in the art of Theatre like Sanskrit drama, Modern Indian drama and traditional Indian Theatre forms.
