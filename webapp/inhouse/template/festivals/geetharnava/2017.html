Geetharnava-2017

The forthcoming Geetharnava will be on the 11th and 12th of March, 2017 at Ganabharathi
(Veene Sheshanna Bhavana), Mysuru.

First day's inaugural concert will be Karnataka classical music by one of India's greatest assets, Sangeetha Kalotthunga,
Dr. R S Nandakumar.

Eminent artists, Vid. Sumana Vedant for Karnataka classical music and Vid. Venkatesh Kulkarni for Hindustani classical music,
will be the Day 2 of Geetharnava.
