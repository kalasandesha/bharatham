Women empowerment

Empowerment of women in our present society, has become a significant topic of discussion in regards to development and economics. It can also point to approaches regarding other trivialized genders in particular social contexts. Kala Sandesha Prathishtana strongly believes that empowerment of women can be aboon for the narional development and has served as mode for achieving it by providing opportunities to women both in terms of employment giving financial stability and education providing social status. Thus most of the employed in Kala Sandesha Prathishtana are women.

Employment

Kala Sandesha Prathishtana is been formulated to create a better world for the artists. The organization is paving way towards this accomplishment by providing employment opportunities to all the completely trained students of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana who are especially from the rural background. These professionally trained people are conducting classes at various branches of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana and thereby are offered jobs for their livelihood.

Youth empowerment

Kala Sandesha Prathishtana also aims at youth empowerment and thereby improve quality of life. Kala Sandesha Prathishtana in this context focuses on processes that enable participation; enhance control through shared decision making; and create opportunities to learn, practice, and increase skills. It is also engaging youth in pro-social, meaningful and community enhancing activities that the youth themselves define and control, helps youth gain vital skills, responsibilities, and confidence necessary to become productive and healthy adults.

Cultural empowerment in rural areas

Being a rural based organization, Kala Sandesha Prathishtana is working very hard . for the appreciation of art in rural areas and the domain of rural development in order to strengthen India and Indian culture from its roots. As an outcome of this Kala Sandesha Prathishtana introduced, undertaken and managed many welfare, religious, educational, social and cultural activities for the benefit of the public at large in rural areas. Kishora Bharatha is one such example for this good deed. Most of the branches of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana are also in rural India to help for the cause.
