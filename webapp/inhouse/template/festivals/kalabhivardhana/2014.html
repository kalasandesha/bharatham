Kalabhivardhana-2014

[With Grant-in-aid from Dept. of Kannada & Culture, Govt. of Karnataka]

Conducted on 5th and 6th of September 2014 at Jaganmohan Palace auditorium Mysore.

Day 1, 5th Sept 2014:

The festival was inaugurated by Dr. Sarvamangala Shankar, Vice-chancellor of Karnataka State, Dr. Gangubai Hanagal Music and Performing Arts University Mysore.We have taken the privilege of honouring National art icon Karnataka Kala Tilaka, the Millennium dancer Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy. During which the title ‘Natya Kaloththunga’ was conferred on Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy, honoured with a purse of Rs. 25,000/-. Followed by her Magnum Bharathanayam Margam performance.

Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy is an A-Grade artist in Dooradarshan. She is the one and only Bharathanatyam Danseuse invited from India to perform for ‘World peace’ in Paris under AEGIS of UNESCO in the presence of 2,500 representatives from 137 countries.

Day 2, 6th Sept 2014:

On the second day of Kalabhivardhana we have taken the privilege of honouring the living legend in the field of Music Dr. R.S. Nandakumar, during which the title ‘Sangeetha Kaloththunga’ was conferred on Dr.R.S. Nandakumar, honoured with a purse of Rs. 25,000/-. Followed by his Soothing concert of Karnataka Sangeetha.

Dr. R.S. Nandakumar is a 4th generation Musician in the parampara of St. Thyagaraja. He is unique in whole of India in resurrecting and reviving the beautiful and ancient wealth of song heritage of more than a thousand years ago. He has done work for UNESCO’s intangible art-forms of Karnataka.

Art Critic Dr.Manik Begeri Mentioned below points in her review published in the daily magazine “Star of Mysore”

  • On the occasion the decennial celebrations of Kala Sandesha Pratishtana this year, the Pratishtana has organized an awards function ‘Kalabhivardhana-2014’, Dr. Vasundhara took the viewers to the world of her creative passion and unbelievable spontaneous choregraphy and her feelings in superb abhinaya.
  • Nandakumar’s expertise in managing scaly variations was stunning. Nandakumar was able reach all the octaves easily which was evident from his singing. It was amazing to hear the rare combinations presented by him.
