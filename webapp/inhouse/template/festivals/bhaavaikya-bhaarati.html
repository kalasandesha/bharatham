Bhaavaikya Bhaarati

Bhaavaikya bhaarati is the annual festival conducted by Bharathadvaya foundation® in the month of august every year with the co-sponsership of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana®. India is a nation with greater heritage and highly versatile cultures. Bhaavaikya bharati is a festival, which intends to promote and propogate indian culture troughout the globe by organizing national integration programs and facilitating talks and discussions about the rich cultural background of India and therby strengthen the roots of indian culture by creating awareness about it amongst the younger generations.
