Nruthyopasana-2013

Conducted on February 23rd and 24th of 2013, organized at Jagan Mohan Palace, Mysore.

Day 1, 23rd Feb 2013

The festival was formally inaugurated by T.R. Harish, Managing Trustee, Vasundhara Performing Arts Center, Mysore, accompanied by Vidhushi Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy, Millennium Artist awarded by UNO, and Gayana Gandharva Dr.R.S. Nandha Kumar, Vocalist, Music Composer, Mysorewhose address to the students was a great inspiration for all of us.The formal function also witnessed the felicitation program of a Music teacher Named Vidwan Kiragasuru Rajappa, Senior Music Teacher, T. Narasipura, who has contributed enough to the music field by teaching music in a rural area like T. Narasipur. For more than Two decades.

The first day program was performance by Vidwan Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav, senior disciple of Karnataka Kala Tilaka Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy.He made his debut on stage proving his inherent gift as a fine dancer. He brings to his dance a rare sense of devotion and dedication,which leaves the audience with a sense of spiritual fulfillment. His strength relieson his ability to co-relate the different disciplines of the art and he has worked withvarious artists – composers, musicians, scholars.

Day 2, 24th Feb 2013

The Second day was the stellar performance of young Artists. The performers here represent the youthful artiste groups, hailing from different backgrounds associated and organized for uphold of culture in younger generations strongly motivated and dedicated towards traditional Bharathanatyam art form.The performance brought the beauty of classical Bharathanatyam.
