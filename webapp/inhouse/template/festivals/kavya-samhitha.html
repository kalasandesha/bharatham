Kavya Samhitha

Kavya Samhitha is a cultural festival organised by Sankeerthana sabha®in the month of June every year with the co-sponsership of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana®. Kavya Samhitha will be a stage for cultural events of Gamakavachana, Bhakthi pantha like Haridasa sahithya, Vachana sahithya, literature of Kabirdas, Tulsidas and Meera. To promote the integration of Kannada literature with different art forms of India.
