Nruthyopasana-2014

Held on 10th, 11th and 12th of May 2014, organized at JaganMohan Palace auditorium, Mysore.

Day 1, 10th May 2014

Event included a Chief Guest of eminence Vidhushi Rama P., eminent Musician, Bangalore; Vidhushi Suneetha Chandrakumar, renowned Musician Mysore; Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy, Bharathanatyam Danseuse Mysore; Dr. R.S. NandhaKumar, Renowned Musicologist, Mysore has consented to be present on the occasion. The festival felicitated Vidhushi Meghala Hirasave, by recognizing her wonderful work. She is a leading performing artist and teacher of Bharatanatyam and Yoga (Ashtanga Vinyasa) in Melbourne, Australia.

The first day program was performance by Vid. Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav, senior disciple of Karnataka Kala Tilaka Dr. Vasundhara Doraswamy. He made his debut on stage proving his inherent gift as a fine dancer. The Culture evening went on with Bharathanatyam Marga performance.

Day 2, 11th May 2014

The Second day was the stellar performance of young talented Artists. Presented a Dance Drama on Goddess Durga, This is composed and choreographed by Guru Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav and performed by nearly 20 young Artists.

Day 3, 12th May 2014

Last is the Platform for Budding Artists (below 14 years). They performed a Dance Drama i.e. “Dhasha Kanaka” which is composed by Kanakadasa, the great revolutionary poet of Kannada. Nearly Seventy Budding artists have shared the stage.

Dr. Manik Begeri, Art Critic Mysore has published her review in daily newspaper ‘Star of Mysore’ as below

  • "Sandesh could bring out the special qualities of Krishna during his childhood through the important incidents like the affection between mother Yashodha and Balakrishna, killing of Putana and playing with his elder brother Balarama"
  • "The presentation got beautified more by presenting ‘Bhandasur Vadha’ in Sanchari Bhaava"
  • "The last day was like an annual gathering of any dance school as more than 90 children between the age group 4 to 15 from all 5 centres of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana participated"
