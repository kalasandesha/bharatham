Nadayana-2016

[Aid from Department of Culture, Karnataka State Government]

Nadayana-2016 was celebrated with the grant in aid from Department of Culture, Karnataka State Government on 10th and 11th of April,2016 at Sri Nadabrahma Sangeeta Sabha, Mysuru.

It was inaugurated by Sangeeta Kalottunga Dr.R.S.Nandakumar who is a renowned Karnataka classical musician of Mysuru. Sri.K.V.Murthy- President of Nadabrahma Sangeetha Sabha and a famous industrialist of Mysore and Smt. Anjana Sureesh Leadership coach at Sri Durga leadership Academy of Mysuru accompanied as Chief guests of the event. Dr.D Umapthy president of KSP , presided over the function.

Vainika Ratna Vidwan Sri R K Padmanabha, well known senior Veena instrumentalist, extraordinarily played Veena on the first day of the festival and was credited with enormous warm wishes from the public.

Vidwan Sri S Rakesh, an upcoming young talent of Mysuru played Flute amazingly , reaching the hearts of the listeners with his electrifying performance on the second day of the festival.
