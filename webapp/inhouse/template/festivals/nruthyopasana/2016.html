Nruthyopasana-2016

[Organised under Grant-in-aid by Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India]

Held on 24th, 25th and 26th of January 2016, organized at JaganMohan Palace auditorium, Mysore.

Day 1 - 24th January,2016

Event included a Chief Guest of eminence Prof Sri Kru. Ramachandra, Renowned Gamaki& Retired Executive Engineer, Mysore; Dr.VasundharaDoraswamy , Bharatanatyam Virtuoso,Mysore; Dr.R.S. NandaKumar, Renowned Musicologist, Mysore; Vid. Sri R. Kannan, Renowned Bharatanatyam Guru, Mysore has consented to be present on the occasion.

The first day program was performance by Vidushi Smt Sindhu Niranjan and Vidushi Kum Anaga M Tsenior Artists of Kala Sandesha Prathishtana. They made his debut on stage proving his inherent gift as a fine dancer. The Culture evening went on with Bharathanatyam Marga performance.

Day 2 - 25th January,2016

The Second day is the Platform for Budding Artists (below 14 years). hey performed Bharathanatyam Marga. Nearly 200 Budding artists from all 19 branches of Prathishtana have shared the stage.

Day 3 - 26th January,2016

Last was the stellar performance of young talented Artists. Presented a Dance Drama on Goddess Durga, This is composed and choreographed by Guru Sri K. Sandesh Bhargav and performed by nearly 20 young Artists.
